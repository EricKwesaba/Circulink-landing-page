About CircuLink Pty Ltd

CircuLink Pty Ltd is dedicated to revolutionizing supply chains through sustainable, technology-driven solutions. Our vision is to create a transparent, efficient, and environmentally responsible ecosystem for electric vehicle parts and logistics in South Africa and beyond.

Vision

To be the leading force in sustainable mobility and circular supply chains, empowering businesses and communities to thrive in a low-carbon future.

Mission

We leverage IoT, blockchain, and data analytics to optimize warehouse management, streamline EV parts distribution, and promote sustainable practices. Our mission is to deliver value through innovation, transparency, and ethical leadership.

Goals

  • Advance the adoption of circular economy principles in supply chains.
  • Enable real-time, data-driven decision making for logistics and warehousing.
  • Support the growth of sustainable mobility in South Africa and Africa at large.
  • Foster partnerships that drive innovation and positive environmental impact.
