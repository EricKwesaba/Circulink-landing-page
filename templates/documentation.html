CircuLink Documentation

Welcome to CircuLink! This documentation provides a comprehensive guide to using the platform, understanding its technical underpinnings, and accessing support. CircuLink is designed to revolutionize sustainable supply chains and EV parts management through blockchain, IoT, and data-driven insights.

Parts Finder Guide

  1. Navigate to Parts Finder from the main menu.
  2. Use filters to search by part category, manufacturer, or warehouse location.
  3. View real-time availability, compare prices, and place orders directly.
  4. Track order status and manage procurement history.

Energy Tracking Guide

  1. Access Energy Tracking from the dashboard.
  2. Monitor energy consumption, waste management, and defect minimization.
  3. Analyze trends with interactive charts and set efficiency targets.
  4. Export data for reporting and compliance.

Efficiency Tracking for Procurement

  1. Compare suppliers on cost, delivery lead times, reliability, and sustainability.
  2. Use the Procurement Optimization tool to identify the best supplier for your needs.
  3. Interactively explore supplier performance data and make informed decisions.

Blockchain Verification

All supplier certifications and energy data are recorded on CircuLink's blockchain for transparency and immutability. Each transaction is hashed and can be verified in the Blockchain Explorer.

// Example: Verifying a supplier certificate hash
GET /api/blockchain?cert_hash=abc123...

// Response
{
  "block": 42,
  "data": {
    "type": "supplier_registration",
    "cert_hash": "abc123..."
  },
  "timestamp": "2024-05-01T12:34:56Z"
}

Sustainability Standards

  • Suppliers must provide valid sustainability certifications (ISO 14001, etc.).
  • Material sourcing and energy use are tracked for environmental compliance.
  • Regular audits ensure ongoing adherence to green standards.

API Integrations

CircuLink offers RESTful APIs for integration with external systems.

// Example: Fetch all suppliers
GET /api/suppliers

// Example: Fetch energy data
GET /api/energy_data

Troubleshooting

  • Login Issues: Ensure your credentials are correct and your account is active.
  • Data Not Updating: Refresh the page or check your network connection.
  • API Errors: Refer to the API documentation for error codes and solutions.

Compliance Policies

  • Data privacy and security are enforced according to POPIA and GDPR standards.
  • All transactions are logged and auditable.
  • Users must adhere to CircuLink's terms of service and ethical guidelines.

User Support

For help, contact our support team at circulinksa@gmail.com or visit the Contact page. You can also explore our Troubleshooting section above.
